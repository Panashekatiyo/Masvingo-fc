Our Legacy

A History of
Passion & Pride

Masvingo United FC is a historic Zimbabwean football club based in the ancient city of Masvingo. Having competed in the Zimbabwe Premier Soccer League — the top tier of Zimbabwean football — our club represents the spirit and determination of our community.


Throughout our journey, we've experienced the highs of championship battles and the challenges of rebuilding. From our sponsorship by Tanda Tavaruva to our partnership with Zimanzi and beyond, we've remained committed to excellence on and off the pitch.


In 2005, we came within inches of the championship, finishing runners-up to CAPS United. Our legacy includes multiple ZIFA Unity Cup victories and Independence Trophy wins.
